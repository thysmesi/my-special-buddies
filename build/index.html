About My Special Buddies

What Is My Special Buddies?

Join us in our mission to ensure that every person with special needs finds so many best friends that they don't know what to do. We offer a platform with a wide variety of potential friends, ensuring that each match is based on shared interests and compatible personalities. Whether you're seeking a friend to share in everyday adventures, someone to listen, or a friend that just gets it, My Special Buddies is your go-to destination for friendships that bring happiness and break barriers.

Safety Is Our Top Priority

Safety, well-being, and compatibility are our priorities.
  • We perform background checks for each potential friend.
  • Our diverse network has a variety of compassionate volunteers, seasoned professionals, and specialists trained to support a spectrum of special needs.
  • Additionally, we recommend doing a secure video call before any in person meetup.

How Does It Work?

Embrace the opportunity to build as many friendships as you want.
  1. Begin by choosing a friend who aligns with your interests.
  2. Message them to find out more
  3. Take the next step with a secure video call to establish a connection.
  4. Once you feel comfortable, you can arrange a meeting at a time and place that suits you both.
